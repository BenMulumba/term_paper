Mission statement

our mission is to provide critical assistance and support
to individuals and families displaced by war in the east of DRC,
offering them hope, dignity, and the resources they need to rebuild their lives.
Join us in transforming lives with your contribution.




Personal story


Alice and her brothers fled Buhumba amid fighting, fleeing to Bugere for fear of mass attacks.
They opened a store and met Elie, who also fled. They settled in Kanyaruchinya,
where their daughter, Rehema Alliance, was born.
she is worried about the future of his child in the current conditions,
as they face armed bandits and demand for money. To meet family needs,
Elie, his father, multiplies small businesses like moto-taxi and coiffeur.
He feels saddened by having his first child in such a harsh environment,
where water freezes when it rains and the sun shines brightly.



Statistics and facts


Over 1 million people have been displaced from their
villages to GOMA TOWN by war since november 2023.
The need for humanitarian assistance is urgent and ongoing.



What is happening in DRC


The Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC) has been grappling
with ongoing conflicts driven by armed rebel groups,
resource exploitation,inter-ethnic tensions,
and political instability. These conflicts have resulted
in a severe humanitarian crisis with millions
displaced and in need of assistance.
Efforts by the government and international
organizations are ongoing to address these challenges,
but the situation remains complex and dynamic.
